Frequently Asked Questions

Is the backpack completely waterproof?

The Urban Explorer is highly water-resistant and will protect your belongings in rain and light water exposure. For complete submersion, we recommend using additional waterproof bags for electronics.

How do I clean my Urban Explorer backpack?

We recommend spot cleaning with mild soap and warm water. For deeper cleaning, hand wash with gentle detergent and air dry completely before use.

What's your warranty policy?

The Urban Explorer comes with a 5-year warranty that covers manufacturing defects. Normal wear and tear is not covered under warranty.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 60 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.
